More Info about VEXCODE V5 Text

In the 2019-2020 season Tower Takover is played on a 12 by 12 foot field with 66 cubes, 22 orange cubes, 22 green cubes, and 22 purple cubes. The goal of the game is to score the most points by scoring cubes in the scoring zones in the corners of the field and also scoring cubes in towers to increase the value of all the cubes by 1 point for both teams.
