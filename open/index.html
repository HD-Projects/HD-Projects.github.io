Open Source Projects

Open source projects are a trye of software projects that the developers release all their code for. The definition of open source according to wikipedia is "Open source products include permission to use the source code,[1] design documents,[2] or content of the product. It most commonly refers to the open-source model, in which open-source software or other products are released under an open-source license as part of the open-source-software movement. Use of the term originated with software, but has expanded beyond the software sector to cover other open content and forms of open collaboration.
